About this activity

Immerse yourself in the rich history of the Silk Road with this comprehensive full-day tour of Samarkand. Known as the "Crossroad of Cultures," Samarkand is one of the oldest inhabited cities in Central Asia. Our expert local guide will take you on a journey through time, from the grandeur of Timur's empire to the vibrant life of the modern city.

Explore the majestic Registan Square, marvel at the azure tiles of the Shah-i-Zinda necropolis, and feel the spiritual atmosphere at the Gur-e-Amir Mausoleum. This tour is perfectly paced to allow for deep appreciation of the architecture while ensuring you see all the major highlights.

Itinerary

9:00 AM - Hotel Pickup

Our driver will meet you in the lobby of your hotel.

9:30 AM - Gur-e-Amir Mausoleum

Start with the final resting place of Amir Timur. A masterpiece of Islamic architecture.

11:00 AM - Registan Square

The heart of ancient Samarkand. We will visit all three madrasahs: Ulugh Beg, Sher-Dor, and Tilya-Kori.

1:00 PM - Lunch Break

Enjoy authentic Samarkand Plov at a renowned local restaurant (Included).

2:30 PM - Shah-i-Zinda Necropolis

Walk through the avenue of mausoleums, famous for their stunning blue tile work.

5:00 PM - Return

Drop off at your hotel or city center.
