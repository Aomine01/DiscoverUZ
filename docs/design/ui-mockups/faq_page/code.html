General Questions

What is Discover Uz?

expand_more

Discover Uz is the premier national platform for booking authentic experiences, tours, and activities across Uzbekistan. We connect international travelers and local residents with verified local guides and tour operators to ensure high-quality adventures.

Is the platform available in multiple languages?

expand_more

Yes! We currently support English, Russian, and Uzbek. You can switch your preferred language using the globe icon in the footer or the settings in your account profile.

Booking & Tickets

How do I receive my ticket?

expand_more

After your booking is confirmed, an electronic ticket (e-ticket) will be sent to your registered email address within 15 minutes. This email will contain a QR code that you can present on your mobile device at the venue or meeting point. No printing required!

Can I book for a large group?

expand_more

Absolutely. For groups larger than 10 people, we recommend contacting our "Group Sales" team directly via the contact form to ensure availability and potentially access special group rates. For smaller groups, you can simply select the number of participants during the standard checkout process.

Do I need to create an account to book?

expand_more

While you can browse tours as a guest, we require account creation at checkout. This ensures your tickets are saved securely and allows you to manage your bookings, download invoices, and contact support easily.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

expand_more

We accept all major international credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, UnionPay) as well as local UzCard and Humo payment systems. All transactions are processed in UZS (Uzbek Soum) or converted automatically by your bank.

Is my payment information secure?

expand_more

Yes, your security is our priority. We use enterprise-grade 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers; payments are processed securely through our regulated banking partners.

Visa & Travel

Do I need a visa for Uzbekistan?

expand_more

Citizens of over 90 countries can enter Uzbekistan visa-free for up to 30 days. Others can easily apply for an e-visa online. We recommend checking the official Ministry of Foreign Affairs website for the most up-to-date requirements for your specific nationality.

Cancellations & Refunds

What is the cancellation policy?

expand_more

Most standard tours offer a full refund if cancelled at least 24 hours before the scheduled start time. However, multi-day tours or special events may have different policies. Please check the specific "Cancellation Policy" section on each tour's booking page before confirming your purchase.

How long do refunds take?

expand_more

Refunds are processed immediately by our system once a cancellation is approved. Depending on your bank or credit card issuer, it typically takes 5-10 business days for the funds to appear on your statement.
