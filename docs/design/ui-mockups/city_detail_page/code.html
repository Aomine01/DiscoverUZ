About the City

Tashkent is the capital of Uzbekistan and a vibrant mix of ancient history and modern life. From the bustling Chorsu Bazaar to the serene Japanese Garden, the city offers a diverse range of experiences for every traveler.

Unlike its desert neighbors Samarkand and Bukhara, Tashkent is a green oasis of parks and fountains. It serves as the main transport hub for the country, making it the perfect starting point for any Uzbek adventure. Discover the heart of Central Asia in a city that never sleeps.

Connectivity

SIM cards are easy to get at the airport. Beeline and Ucell offer good tourist packages with 4G data.

Dining Etiquette

Plov is usually eaten for lunch. Tipping 10-15% is standard in restaurants if service charge isn't included.

Safety

Tashkent is very safe for tourists. Tourist Police operate in major areas and speak English.
